👨‍💼 HR Analytics Employee Attrition Analysis Dashboard
📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing employee attrition patterns using SQL, Python, and Power BI. The objective is to identify the factors influencing employee turnover and provide actionable insights to improve employee retention and workforce management.
The project combines data extraction, cleaning, exploratory data analysis, and interactive dashboard development to support data-driven HR decision-making.
________________________________________
🎯 Business Problem
Employee attrition is a major challenge for organizations as it increases recruitment costs, affects productivity, and impacts business performance. Understanding the reasons behind employee turnover helps organizations develop effective retention strategies.

🔍 Key Insights
•	Research & Development department recorded the highest attrition.
•	Laboratory Technicians showed the highest employee turnover.
•	Employees with lower salaries were more likely to leave.
•	Younger employees contributed significantly to attrition.
•	Lower job satisfaction was associated with higher turnover rates.
•	Male employees represented the majority of the workforce.
